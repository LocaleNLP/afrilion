# AfriLION Recruitment Channels & Strategy

## 1. Academic Channels (High Authority)
* **University of Dakar (UCAD):** NLP research groups and linguistic departments.
* **Makerere University (Mak-AI):** Focus on Luganda and East African languages.
* **University of the Witwatersrand (Wits):** South African language specialists.
* **Addis Ababa University:** Amharic and Ge'ez script experts.

## 2. Community & Grassroots
* **Masakhane Slack:** The primary channel for African NLP researchers.
* **Zindi Africa:** Engaging data scientists through competitive data collection challenges.
* **Deep Learning Indaba:** Annual gathering for networking with top African AI talent.
* **Regional Tech Hubs:** iHub (Nairobi), CcHub (Lagos), Technopark (Casablanca).

## 3. Social Media Strategy
* **"Friday Tweet" Campaign:** Weekly updates on dataset growth to attract volunteer and paid contributors.
* **LinkedIn Thought Leadership:** Long-form posts on "The Wolof Pattern" and "Built IN Africa" philosophy.
* **Reddit r/LanguageLearning & r/MachineLearning:** Targeted threads for specific language pairs.

## 4. Referral Program
* **"Creator-to-Creator" Incentives:** Bonus tokens or stipends for coordinators who bring in verified linguistic experts from their personal networks.
