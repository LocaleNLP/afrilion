# AfriLION Regional Coordinator: Role Specification

## Overview
The Regional Coordinator is the bridge between the technical development team and the local linguistic communities. This role is pivotal for the "Wolof Pattern" of data creation.

## Key Responsibilities
* **Network Building:** Recruit and manage a team of 10-20 "Language Creators" within your linguistic region.
* **Quality Assurance:** Audit human-created datasets for linguistic accuracy, cultural nuances, and bias.
* **Technical Onboarding:** Train local creators on using Label Studio and GitHub for data submission.
* **Community Advocacy:** Represent AfriLION at local university labs and tech meetups.

## Requirements
* **Linguistic Mastery:** Native or near-native fluency in at least one targeted African language (e.g., Wolof, Swahili, Amharic, Yoruba).
* **Technical Literacy:** Comfort with Git, Markdown, and basic Python (or a strong willingness to learn).
* **Community Leadership:** Proven experience in organizing workshops or leading student groups.
* **Location:** Must be based in a regional hub (Dakar, Casablanca, Nairobi, Lagos, or Addis Ababa).

## Compensation & Perks
* Monthly stipend (funded via grants).
* Access to premium compute resources for personal research projects.
* Direct mentorship from LocaleNLP core engineers.
* Co-authorship on research papers and dataset releases.
