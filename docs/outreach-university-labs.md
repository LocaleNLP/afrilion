Dear EthioNLP / HiLT Lab Team,

I am Alieu Jagne, lead researcher on AfriLION — an open-source initiative building NLP infrastructure for African languages. Amharic is one of our five priority languages for Phase 1 (alongside Swahili, Hausa, Yoruba, and Wolof), and your work on Amharic NER, POS tagging, and the Amharic corpus is foundational to our pipeline.

We are processing the CC-100 Amharic subset (862MB) through our quality pipeline — langdetect confidence filtering, MinHash LSH deduplication, and length filtering — and will publish the cleaned corpus on Hugging Face under an open license.

**We would like to explore:**
1. Cross-validation of our cleaned Amharic data against your existing corpora
2. Collaboration on Amharic-specific tokenization (Ethiopic script challenges)
3. Including your NER/POS datasets in our AfriLION evaluation suite
4. Co-authorship on Amharic language modeling results
5. Connecting Addis Ababa University students with our open-source project
